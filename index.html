Welcome to Tutorial 4

Exploring Image Tracking with MindAR and Three.js

What is Tutorial 4?

Tutorial 4 demonstrates an **Augmented Reality (AR)** image tracking experience. Using the MindAR library, this project detects a specific target image ("Light of the World") and overlays a synchronized video player directly onto the physical target in real-time.

  • Interactive Playback: Video starts automatically when the target is found.
  • Auto-Pause: The video pauses when the camera loses the target to save resources.
  • Three.js Integration: High-performance 3D rendering for smooth visuals.
